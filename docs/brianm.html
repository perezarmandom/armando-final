Brian May

Brian Harold May, CBE is an English musician, singer, songwriter, producer and astrophysicist. He is the lead guitarist of the rock band Queen. His songs include 'We Will Rock You', 'Tie Your Mother Down', 'I Want It All', 'Fat Bottomed Girls', 'Flash', 'Hammer to Fall', 'Save Me', 'Who Wants to Live Forever' and 'The Show Must Go On'.
